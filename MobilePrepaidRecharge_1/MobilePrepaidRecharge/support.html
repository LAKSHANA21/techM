Frequently Asked Questions

To activate your new SIM card, simply insert it into your phone and follow the on-screen instructions. If you need assistance, you can call our 24/7 support line or visit your nearest MOBI-COMM store.

If you experience network issues, try these steps:
  • Restart your device
  • Check if airplane mode is off
  • Update your network settings
  • Contact our support if the issue persists

You can check your data usage through:
  • MOBI-COMM mobile app
  • Your account dashboard
  • Dial *123# from your phone
  • SMS 'USAGE' to 12345
